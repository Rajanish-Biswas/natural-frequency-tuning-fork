What does Dominant Freq / Harmonic / Inference tell you?

Why you need it — the picture it shows

Your vibration meter gives broadband velocity (mm/s) and displacement (µm). These numbers tell you HOW MUCH vibration — but not WHY. The dominant frequency tells you WHERE the energy is concentrated, which points to the mechanical cause.
